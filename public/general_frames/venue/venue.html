Community Guidelines


  1. Be respectful to those around you

    2. In order to create a safe space among attendees, please be aware of your surroundings and respect the personal space of those around you.


  2. No weapons are allowed

    3. Items such as guns, knives, pepper spray, or any object that can be used as a weapon are explicitly banned.


  3. Try to provide help to those who may need it

    4. If someone near you is in distress or requires medical attention, please do what you can to get them the help they need.
